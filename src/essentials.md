## Essentials
Colors are as such:
1. Player (Sova) - A lil dark blue
2. Phoenix - A kinda orange-yellow
3. Obstacles - Super red
4. Collectibles - Bright gold
5. Warning lanes - orange
6. danger lanes - red

Glow implementation:
Soft Glow - Interactive
Hard glow - Danger or combat

Motions
Static = safef/none
pulsing = warning
shaking = danger
flash = impact
